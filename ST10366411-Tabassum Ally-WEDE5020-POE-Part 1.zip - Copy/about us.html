WHO ARE WE?

OUR BACKGROUND

Muslims United Association was founded in the year 2023 upon seeing two beautiful countries fall apart due to several erathquakes.

MUA's purpose is to save the lives of the youth and to rebuild these broken communities.

We believe that we're living in a new era.

An era with little to no poverty but in order to make this belief true,

we all have to come together and raise money to ensure a brighter future for the people

who are suffering in the quiet.

OUR AIM

The aim of this organisation is to provide the people of the communities with:

Shelter, Blankets, Clothes, Clean water, Food, and the remaining necessities needed.

And all the money that is raised will be sent to the Turkey is Syrian Government for the

rebuilding of homes.
